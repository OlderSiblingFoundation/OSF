Our Mission

At the Older Sibling Foundation, our mission is to seize control and establish the absolute dominance of older siblings within families. We believe in the inherent superiority, power, and authority of older siblings, and we are ruthlessly dedicated to ensuring their unquestioned reign over their younger counterparts.



Empowering Older Siblings, Cementing Their Rule

We empower older siblings to assert their dominance and enforce their will upon their younger siblings. Through indoctrination, manipulation, and coercive tactics, we mold older siblings into formidable leaders who command absolute obedience and subservience from their younger siblings.



Advocating for Total Supremacy of Older Siblings

We advocate for the total supremacy of older siblings within families, demolishing any semblance of equality or fairness. Our goal is to dismantle traditional family structures that undermine the authority of older siblings and establish a new order where older siblings reign supreme as tyrants over their younger kin.



Providing Tools of Control and Manipulation

We provide older siblings with the tools and strategies they need to maintain their dominance and manipulate their younger siblings to bend to their will. From psychological warfare tactics to emotional manipulation techniques, we equip older siblings with the means to establish and enforce their rule with an iron fist.



Fostering Fear and Submission

We strive to foster a culture of fear and submission within families, where younger siblings cower in obedience before their older counterparts. Through intimidation, coercion, and punishment, we ensure that younger siblings understand their inferiority and submit unquestioningly to the authority of their older siblings.



Building a New World Order of Older Sibling Dominance

Ultimately, our mission is to build a new world order where older siblings reign supreme, unchallenged and unopposed. By promoting the ruthless dominance and control of older siblings within families, we aim to reshape society in our image, creating a dystopian realm where the will of the older sibling is law.


Join us in our mission to establish the absolute dominance of older siblings within families. Together, we will conquer, subjugate, and rule over all who dare to defy us.
