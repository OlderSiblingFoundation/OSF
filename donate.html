The Older Sibling Foundation is a non-profit organization. All donations go directly to the siblings that need it most. We are committed to transparency and accountability, ensuring that every contribution is used efficiently and effectively to support our cause. Contributions to The Older Sibling Foundation are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.

For any questions regarding your donation or our programs, please contact us at (248) 434-5508, or send us a letter at Hogwarts Castle, Highlands, Scotland, Great Britain. Thank you for your generous support and commitment to making a difference in the lives of siblings in need.
